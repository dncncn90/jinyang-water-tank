# Water Tank Website - Site Vision

## Overview
A fast, intuitive, and highly professional online quotation system for industrial and residential water tanks.
The target demographic is primarily Korean men in their 30s-60s engaged in construction, plumbing, or facility management.

## Key Design Principles
1. **Trust and Professionalism**: Dark blue colors, sharp corners, bold typography.
2. **Speed and Efficiency**: Clear CTA buttons, minimal steps, fast "1-minute AI estimate" process.
3. **High Legibility**: Large text, high contrast, avoiding cluttered designs.
